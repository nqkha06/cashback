Our Story

Backcash started with a simple observation: affiliate marketing generates billions of dollars for publishers, but the actual shoppers—the people spending the money—rarely see a dime of it.

We thought, "What if we just split the commission with the shopper?"

By partnering directly with thousands of stores, we aggregate our massive audience's buying power to negotiate higher commission rates. When you shop through our links, stores pay us for sending them a customer. We take a small cut to keep the lights on, and send the majority of that money right back to you as cashback.
